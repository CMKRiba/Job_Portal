Online English Teacher

South Africa Online R100 - R150 per hour

Job description

Looking for a flexible online teaching opportunity? Want to manage your own time? We are seeking for responsible and passionate educators to teach English online to over 500,000 students in China – with the flexibility to teach from anywhere.

Benefits

  • Hours assigned as soon as you finish your training, no need to wait weeks to get hours assigned.
  • Students are welcomed! No experience required* Willing to learn*
  • Regular Students every week (stable monthly income) Guaranteed*
  • Part time - You can manage your schedule and weekly teaching hours.
  • Lessons provided

Requirements

  • A Bachelor’s Degree in any field – Preference given to degrees in Language and Education; or any related teaching certificate (TESOL, TEFL, etc.)
  • Available to teach Part time or Full time (Afternoon to Evening Beijing time)(2 pm - 11 pm BJT)***
  • Reliable Computer (Desktop or Laptop) Our Software does not accept Chromebooks
  • Internet access (Wired connection using cable - preferred)
  • Commitment to teaching minimum of one complete semester (6 months)
